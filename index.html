Our coffee is roasted in a hot air, refractory lined roaster with an extraordinary level of control. The significance of relying on a high volume of hot air to roast coffee beans can't be understat- ed. Most small coffee roasters use a single stage burner which offers virtually no control during the roasting process. The refrac- tory significantly reduces hot spots which can damage coffee beans and produces a much more even roasting environment.
